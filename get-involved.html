Volunteering with YSMF

Young Strong Mothers Foundation (YSMF) stands as a home of hope, solutions and inspiration for vulnerable teens and young mothers aged 11 to 24. We have various programs which give them an opportunity to rebuild their lost dreams and become strong women.

YSMF welcomes individuals and organizations who want to join our effort through investing their time, skills or resources to give a second chance to the teen and young mothers registered in YSMF. These are unpaid volunteers positions, 2-10 hours, 2-6 month, rural and urban areas. We need volunteers for various assignments including:

  • Psychological counselling and motivation

    Teens and young mothers face many challenges beside their age. Living without family, social or government support and yet have no idea of parenthood, have no educations or skills to be employed or self employed. All these and more, make them live under stress, fear, shame and trauma. Psychological counselling and motivation talks help them feel welcomed and ready to start again. That is why we are looking for a psychological counsellors to invest his/her time and skills to offer this support so that teens and young mothers can welcome the opportunities YSMF is finding for them with clear mind.

  • Training on new handmade skills

    Teens and young mothers needs various skills which take short period of time and yet can help them gain skills for employment, self employed and business to help them create a source of income to support their children and their basic needs. Apart from tailoring skills, we need more handmade skills to help them stand on their own.

  • Design and media

    YSMF is seeking for design and media volunteers who can help us with website maintenance and data updating, brochure and flyer graphic design, social media set up, impact report graphic design and marketing strategy.

If you are interested and have the skills mentioned or any skills that are suitable for teens and young mothers, please apply by filling the form below.
