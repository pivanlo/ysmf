The home of hope and inspiration

Our vision

To become a home of hope, inspirations and solutions to teen mothers, girl child and women in Tanzania mainland.

Our mission

To provide a second chance to young (teen mothers) who are school dropout to return to school after birth to finish their education cycle which was vandalized by teen pregnancy, find and raise their talents and skills as well as to support through empowerment programs which can help them to have source of income generating. To provide counseling and awareness education on teen pregnancies, sexual diseases including HIV/AIDS as well as leadership skills for creating confidence and feeling of belonging to re-building their childhood lost dreams of their future.
