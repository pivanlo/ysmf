What we do

We mainly focus on:

  • Advocacy and awareness on gender based violence and women’s rights

  • Education empowerment mainly in vocational skills

  • Economic empowerment mainly in entrepreneurship skills

  • Promoting access to right information for awareness on health issues mainly focusing on mental health support, counselling, and sexual and reproductive health

  • Child program mainly focusing on child care for children of young mothers
